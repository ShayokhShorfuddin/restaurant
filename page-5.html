How Foodland Works for Restaurant Partners

1

With Active Restaurant, restaurant owners can efficiently manage customer orders through a unified platform. Once a customer places an order via the website or mobile app, restaurant owners are instantly notified.

2

In the kitchen, staff can prepare food accurately according to customer specifications. The system is designed to ensure seamless communication between the order and the preparation process.

3

The platform alerts you when the delivery personnel are on their way to pick up the order, making it easy to pack and hand over meals quickly with streamlined coordination between the restaurant and the delivery boy.
